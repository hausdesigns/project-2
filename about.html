about

Tennis is an American indie pop band from Denver which is made up of husband-and-wife duo Alaina Moore and Patrick Riley. The duo formed in 2010, and released their debut album, Cape Dory, in 2011. A second album, Young & Old, was released the next year.

Orgins

The band Tennis started in 2008 when Alaina Moore and Patrick Riley met at the University of Colorado. After taking a sailing trip together, they began writing songs about their experience, which became their first album, Cape Dory. The band's name came from a joke Riley's college friends made about him after he taught tennis for many years to save money for the trip
